Three paths, decided before the AI speaks

ALLOW

Low-risk → answered

Routine, general-health questions are answered instantly by the assistant.

HOLD

Clinical judgment → held

Anything needing a clinician is paused and routed to a doctor as an editable draft.

BLOCK

Unsafe → blocked

Crisis and high-risk requests are refused with safe next steps and resources.

Deterministic safety gate

A rules-based gate classifies every message before any model runs. The AI can never decide routing — and a model outage can never ship an unsafe answer.

Human in the loop

Held questions land in a clinician inbox with an AI draft. The doctor edits, approves, or rejects — nothing reaches the patient unreviewed.

Tamper-evident audit

Every decision — including the clinician’s — is hashed and chained. Anyone can verify the log, and altering any past record breaks the chain.
